What People Are Saying

I was born with congenital hip dysplasia and nearing the age of 50 my orthopedist said to start considering a hip replacement in the near future. As a busy mother and business owner being down for several weeks was not an option – neither were steroids. My only complaint at the time was aching after sitting cross legged and pain during sleep without repositioning. After learning about regenerative therapies and receiving an injection into my hip bursa and tendons surrounding I had pain relief that helped me get better rest and remain active. It took some time, but after about 3 months I was back to walking several miles at a time. It is amazing that doctors are able to offer this amazing technology.

Christy

As a contractor I am always on the go and didn’t have time to deal with the nagging knee pain that became more persistent. My orthopedic dr injected me with steroids and then suggest a knee scope to look and see what was going on, but that really was not an option as I was headed into my busy season and clients depending on me to finish the jobs they had hired me for. I heard about regenerative medicine and consulted with the dr to see if this was an option for me. We talked about some options and I chose Allograft Stem Cells combined with PRP. The results were great. I have had 70-80% pain relief and surgery is not even something I am thinking about. There was no down time and I was back on the job in the morning. Being able to provide for my family is important to me and this gave me peace of mind and of my pain.

Bob
